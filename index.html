—WHAT'S NEXT—

Our expectations on what we think will happen in the future (next school year) reads:



Next year in ICT, we anticipate learning more advanced coding languages. We expect to create more complex websites that will feature a greater amount of information and content. Emotionally, we believe it will be challenging at first, but we’re confident that we’ll adapt—similar to when we first began learning ICT. It was difficult initially, but with our genuine passion, adjusting won’t be too hard.

With utmost sincerity, we are genuinely excited to learn and create even more in ICT next year. As we continue to grow and improve, we know that by the end of the next school year, we might look back at this website and realize how far we've come—perhaps even thinking it wasn't very good at all. But that’s a sign of progress. We look forward to developing our skills further, embracing more advanced tools and concepts, and building projects that reflect our growth, creativity, and dedication. Our hope is to keep learning, keep improving, and create something we can truly be proud of.
